What is the first thing you think about when someone says they're from Brazil?

I'm going to guess: soccer, carnaval, and... beaches? Well, I don't know if that was what you thought about, but those are the most famous stereotypes I've heard from people from other countries about Brazil.
As a Brazilian, I must say that that's not even wrong. We had great soccer players like Pelé, Ronaldinho Gaúcho, and Ronaldo Fenômeno. Nowadays, people talk a lot about Neymar ~super overrated in my opinion~ who quickly got famous for having the Brazilian way of dribbling rhythmically and making his professional debut at only 17 years old.
Carnaval is a really big celebration and several people put a lot of effort into creating themes every year to guide their whole parade. Everything needs to be connected, they create songs called "enredos" and those songs need to talk about the theme they chose, all the costumes and the cars they use during the parade needs to follow the same idea. The themes are usually something relevant that is happening or has happened in Brazil or in the world. They explore themes like history, culture and social issues.
Oh and there's also the beaches... and do I agree with that being something to think about when Brazil is mentioned? YES! I love the beaches. I'm speaking on a point of view of a person who hasn't even visited 20% of the main beaches in Brasil, but the ones I've been to and specifically the ones in my region, Northeast... I can assure you, we do have amazing beaches.
Are we talking about looks? Yes! They're stunning, but...
Going to the beach in Brasil is always a fun and crazy experience.
Urban beaches usually have restaurants that will serve you while you're relaxing in front of the sea, under the shade and chairs that they usually provide. Another option is renting a beach umbrella or bringing your own. Another good idea would be to bring your own food as well, however, there's always people walking around selling all you can think about: bbq, sea food, drinks, popsicles, ice cream, towels, bathing suits, sunscream, stews, sunglasses, handcrafted earings, necklesses, rings, and so much more.
